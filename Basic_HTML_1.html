What are two ways to cook a steak?


Searing


One way to cook a steak is by Searing. In order to sear a steak, you:
- Cook for short time steak on very high heat in pan or on hot side of a grill
- Move steak to oven or cooler side of grill until internal temp reaches 125-130ºF
- Remove steak and let rest for 10 minutes before slicing

Reverse-Searing


Another way to cook a steak is by Reverse-Searing. In order to reverse-sear a steak, you:
- Cook steak in oven or cooler side of grill until internal temp reaches 125-130ºF
- Move steak to high heat pan or hot side of a grill for short time
- Remove steak and let rest for 10 minutes before slicing
